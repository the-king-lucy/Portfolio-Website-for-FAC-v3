Why I Want To Do This

To Investigate

From interpreting Satellite Imagery to APIs and web scraping, learning to code will enable me to do much more complex investigations

To Make Interactives

Learning Javascript would mean I have the tools to present and publish my work

For Data

Here's What Has Inspired Me

Who I am

I'm currently a TV Producer / Journalist

I began my career as a TV producer, working mostly on documentaries. I then moved into video-journalism and for the last 3 years have worked for the New York Times. I left the NYT in December so I could dedicate 6 months to learning to code.

Why? Well, over the last few years I've come up against so many (digital) brick walls in my reporting.

Maybe I was trying to investigate a government agency and saw they had an API but I didn't know what to do with it. Or I wanted to create an interactive to help the public understand a complicated piece of legislation. Or I would hear colleagues talking about scraping or using React.js or Svelte...

There's been so many of these moments over the last few years. I'm so enthusiastic about learning this magical language so I can be a much more useful investigative reporter.

So, here's hoping you're help me become a Journocoder!
